Latest Release

For My Love

For My Love, recorded at Sugar House is our latest release in February of 2021. This is another song we have taken great pride & enjoyment in writing and recording; which incedentally is our first song to be recorded professionally at a studio.

Indigo Who?

After playing BBC Introducing sessions and selling out in HMV, Grimsby Indig rockers, Indigo Bay, launch their first professionally recorded release 'For My Love'. Recorded by sugar House and mastered by Robin Schmidt, they land with a pleading message addressing toxic masculinity, bringing their ethereal guitar melodies, pumping drum loops and dreamy vocals. This project aims to get men to address their feelings through 1-1 discussions whilst providing a sonic escape.
