Latest Release

Bleach

This song represents how we can sometimes end up fighting our own mind for the mental freedom we all want. When we look back to our childhood when we didn't have to worry about so much. This song has been moulded from our own experiences and battles and hope that it can alleviate some of the issues you may be facing in your daily life. We recorded this song at Sugar House Music in Saint Helens. The artwork was made by the amazing Emily Diane Lund.

Indigo Who?

After playing BBC Introducing sessions and selling out in HMV, Grimsby Indie rockers, Indigo Bay, launch their first professionally recorded release 'For My Love'. Recorded by Sugar House and mastered by Robin Schmidt, they land with a pleading message addressing toxic masculinity, bringing their ethereal guitar melodies, pumping drum loops and dreamy vocals. This project aims to get men to address their feelings through 1-1 discussions whilst providing a sonic escape.
